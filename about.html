Welcome to Salon Oranje's website

Welcome to Salon Oranje, a beauty parlour and men's barber shop located in the heart of Sasolburg, proudly run by two sisters. We are excited to have recently re-opened our business, and we have been delighted to have already welcomed so many satisfied customers.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide the best quality beauty and grooming services in Sasolburg, and we strive to make each and every visit to our salon a memorable experience. Our team of experienced professionals are highly trained in the latest trends and techniques and are committed to providing you with the best possible service. We offer a wide range of services, from haircuts and styling to colouring, perming and facial and body treatments. We also provide an array of hair care products and treatments to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.At Salon Oranje, we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our customers. We understand that every person has their own unique style, and we are committed to helping you look and feel your best. We look forward to seeing you soon!
