Welcome to Salon Oranje's website

Welcome to Salon Oranje! We are so delighted to have opened our doors once again and to have the opportunity to serve the Sasolburg community. We are Bapsi and Charlene two sisters who own and operate the salon and barber shop, we have put our hearts and souls into creating the beauty parlour and barber shop that brings out the best in everyone. We look forward to providing you our clients with an inviting and relaxing atmosphere, and to helping you look and feel your very best!

come and meet us

At Salon Oranje, we offer an extensive range of services to help you look and feel your best. Our team of experienced professionals are dedicated to providing you with a truly luxurious experience. From haircuts and styling to manicures, pedicures, and facials, we have something for everyone. We invite you to come in and experience the difference for yourself. And if you're short on time, you can even make an appointment or explore our website to find out more. So come and visit us at Salon Oranje - we can't wait to meet you!
